Guided Pipeline: First Mobile Sales Phase

This phase turns the mobile app into a field-sales workflow, not just a smaller copy of the web app.

Primary Flow
1. Customer List
2. Customer Detail: contact, balance, recent quotes/orders
3. Quotation Form: customer, products, quantities, totals
4. Order Detail: status, items, payment summary
Supporting Screens
Product picker with search and stock visibility
Line item editor: quantity, price, discount
Payment form: amount, method, reference, date
Reusable detail sections for customers, quotes, orders

Phase 1 Scope

1

Customer detail

Tap customer list cards to inspect customer data, recent quotations, recent orders, and balances.

2

Create quotation

Mobile form with product picker, line items, totals, and submit.

3

Convert quotation to order

Use the backend conversion endpoint already mirrored by the web service layer.

4

Record payment

Add payment capture from order detail with method/reference/date/amount.
